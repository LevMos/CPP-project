This program simulates survey. User can fill questionnaire with Respondent`s age, gender, education, and answer - YES or NO
Based on questionnaire answers program can divide Respondents into 3 groups:
  Male respondents over 40 with higher education who answered YES
  Female respondents under 30 with middle education who answered NO
  Male respondents under 25 with primary education who answered YES

Also program can show all filled questionnairies
